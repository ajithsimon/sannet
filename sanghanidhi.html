Functional features:

  • Compatible with all micro finance models(SHG,JLG,grameen,Individuals and hybrid)
  • Lending from multi sources to the same portfolio of clients and generating source wise reports ,books of accounts and statements.
  • Geographical information collectdd using 5 tier model to facilitate state wise,district wise,blockwise ,location wise and client wise reports.
  • User definable loan products (size of loan,repayment period ,installments ,rate of interest, interest calculation method,limits and ceilings and penal provisions)

Loan Analysis :

  • Demand ,Collection,Balance (DCB) analysis at various levels
  • Ageing Analysis,PAR and PIA for 30,60,90,180,365 and above 365
  • Area wise,Purpose wise ,loan product wise,borrowing wise and staff wise analysis and abstracts
  • Graphical analysis of DCB,overdues PAR and PIA
  • Efficiency ,profitability and performancy ratios
  • DashBoard reports on multiple financial indicators

Financial Statement of Accounts Books of Accounts:

  • Loan ledger for individual clients and group of clients
  • Cash book, bank book and loan book
  • Receipts and payments account ,income and expenditure account and balance sheet(branch wise and or source wise)
  • Portfolio abstract on par with CGAP standards
  • Accounting of micro finance transactions at various levels with facility for consolidation of accounts and statements.
