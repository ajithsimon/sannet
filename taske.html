  • TASKe App

Virtual management of Tasks can help fast track completion of tasks and activities regardless of Square pegs and Round holes, using TASKe App.

Download and subscribe for your team members to instantly plan a task using smart phones, update while driving or having a coffee, upload a live photo to confirm the progress and keep interact with status updates.

More than a task organizer, it helps to track the GPS location of the taskie(whom the task is assigned) makes the task management reliable.

TASKe APP is designed to manage tasks assigned between team members using smart phones eliminating emails and calls. Vital information of their locations can be tracked to confirm the progress and propriety of their locations.

Using smart phone one can assign a task with instructions to their team members. There will be a notification to the person to whom it is assigned . The task should be first accepted as a token of confirmation. On acceptance it is notified to the tasker(one who assigns the task).

Status update can be done by both the parties i.e tasker and taskie. TASK dial displays the latest update and photos if any uploaded to evidence such progress. On completion of each task the taskie updates the completion with visual evidence and it is notified to the tasker.

TASK dial has blue, orange, red and green colours to distinguish the pending activities with red, yellow for progressing and green for completion in time.

TASKe helps in being conscious of time line, location tracking, photo proof of progress and also to document various discussions taken place from assigning a task to completion. Manage your tasks and activies using TASKe App that keeps you updated with status, locations and visuals.
