Dear CA professional,

As you are aware, the recently introduced GST in the country has presented a business opportunity of more than Rs 36,000 crores for the software industry in migration of existing data and new users into this domain.

In light of this situation, GSTbooks, ERP and on-line accounting solution franchisee is offered to the CA community as re-distributor of the software.

GSTbooks is in the cloud hosted from our servers in California. SME version is priced at Rs.1,725/- ( with GST) per annum for one BUSINESS ENTITY ( per GSTN) and for single branch.

Franchisee is offered in a package of 30 SME version subscription at Rs.30,000/- one time payment. Franchisee will have 30% margin on future renewals and additional users above the fixed 30 SME users.

Your team will be initially trained and thereafter you have to train your clients and handhold the implementation.

Mobile based interfaces (mobile app) will be provided for select functions and reports.

Users can be created by the admin user of your client with assigned rights and privileges.

Send your queries through this link http://gstbooks.in/query.aspx

On-line registration of your Expression Of Interest (EOI) can be submitted using this link. http://gstbooks.in/caeoi.aspx
