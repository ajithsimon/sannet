Main Features

GST CHART

GST tax rates and code structure is adopted along with sub-categories that are not introduced rater. Since July 2017 to till date the GST returns are prepared without hassles.

PURCHASE ORDER

PO raising ,GRN-goods receipting,invoice processing and payment of invoice are built-in with maker-checker internal control basis, ensures the accounting is accurate and on par with standards .

SALES INVOICE

Auto mark-up, stock status, back order , delivery docket generation makes the invoicing user friendly. Collections against each invoice, Credit Notes , Sales returns and credit setting are salient features.

SERVICE INVOICE

Service rate master based billing of services rendered to customers, provision for comments and setting of terms and conditions makes the service invoice preparation easy.

GST RETURNS

GSTR 1-11 can be generated without any hassles and there is not need for reconciliation. Jason files can be prepared and uploaded without any technical difficulties.

E-WAY BILLING

On-line preparation of E Way bills facilitated . Options for multi invoices transported in one vehilce and single invoice transported in multiple vehicles .

JOBS INWARD AND OUTWARD

Inwards job orders can be captured with materials and job rates. Invoicing on completion of job works on the inward job rates makes the invoicing very simple.

INVENTORY ACCOUNTING

Quantitative stock accounting maintained for all sales and purchases except general invoicing. Stock valuation on FIFO method and stock adjustment entries for stock taking makes it perfect inventory accounting.

FINANCIAL STATEMENTS & REPORTS

Trial Balance, Profit and Loss account, Balance Sheet, Customer accounts, Supplier Accounts, Stock ledger and ledger accounts are available . Ageing report, Daily stock report, cash report and various other reports are generated.
